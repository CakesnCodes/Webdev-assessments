Epithet Erased is an action-comedy series created by Brendan "JelloApocalypse" Blaber on YouTube.

It is set in a world where one out of every five people are born with an 'Epithet', a word that is inscribed onto their very soul that grants them various abilities depending on to word. Epithets are random, meaning one can have common words like ★Soup★ or ★Barrier★ to rare ones like ★Parapet★ or ★Augment★, however, it's up to the users to train these epithets to become powerful

The series began as a collection of 7 animated episodes released on VRV (and later released on YouTube) in 2019, with future intallments, such as Prison of Plastic, coming in the form of Light Novels, and Audiobooks
