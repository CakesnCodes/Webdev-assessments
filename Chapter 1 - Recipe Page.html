Ponyo's Honey Milk

This is a lovely drink recipe that I found while scouring the internet, based on a scene from one of my favorite films of all time 'Ponyo',
I wasn't capable of finding the original creator of the recipe, however, it's a rather simple drink that anyone could make that is perfect for cold rainy weather, just like in the movie!

Ponyo written and directed by Hayao Miyazaki and animated by Studio Ghibli

Ingredients

  • Honey- 2 tsp
  • Cinnamon
  • Hot Water
  • Milk

Instructions

  1. In a mug, pour 2 tsp of honey and a sprinkle of cinnamon
  2. Pour hot water halfway into mug
  3. Fill rest of the mug with milk
  4. Stir and enjoy!
